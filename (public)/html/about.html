About Carib Scholar

Carib Scholar was founded with a mission to revolutionize CSEC exam preparation across the Caribbean. We understand the unique challenges students face in accessing quality study materials and personalized feedback. Our platform combines a comprehensive database of past papers with the power of AI to provide an unparalleled learning experience.

Our AI-powered marking system, built on the Gemini model, gives you instant, detailed feedback on your answers, helping you understand not just what you got wrong, but why. With our progress tracking and subject filtering, you can pinpoint your weak areas and focus your study time where it matters most. We are committed to helping every Caribbean student achieve academic excellence.
